# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

## Overview

OpenPostings is an open-source application for viewing job postings, tracking applications, and managing user-defined job search settings.

OpenPostings is local-first. The app runs on your device. OpenPostings maintainers do not run a central user account system for this app and do not operate a hosted user database for your profile/application records.

## Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores data locally on your device when you use app features, including:

- Information you choose to enter in the app (for example: profile fields, application notes, and settings).
- Job posting data retrieved from public employer/ATS endpoints.
- Operational sync metadata (for example: last sync time and sync status).

## No Data Collection by OpenPostings Maintainers

OpenPostings maintainers do not receive your personal app data.

- No required account signup with OpenPostings maintainers.
- No OpenPostings-hosted user database for your jobs/profile/application records.
- No OpenPostings-owned analytics/telemetry pipeline for personal app records.
- No sale of personal data.

## What the App Uses Local Data For

- Showing and filtering job postings.
- Saving your application-tracking records.
- Running optional background sync features.
- Import/export and migration of your local settings/data.

## Storage and Retention

- OpenPostings stores data locally on your device/computer by default.
- You can delete app data by uninstalling or clearing app storage, and by deleting exported files you created.
- OpenPostings does not require creation of a hosted OpenPostings account.

## Data Leaves Your Device Only If You Choose

- Your local profile/application data stays on your device by default.
- Data leaves your device only when you explicitly export/share it or route it to infrastructure you control.

## Permissions

Depending on platform/version, OpenPostings may request permissions such as:

- Network access (to fetch job postings/sync content from external endpoints).
- Notifications (for background sync status).
- File access (for import/export workflows).

## Third-Party Endpoints

When syncing/fetching postings, requests are made from your device/runtime to employer/ATS endpoints (or to endpoints you configure). Those third-party services operate under their own privacy policies and terms.

## Children

OpenPostings is not directed to children under 13.

This policy may be updated over time. Updated versions will be posted at this same URL.
